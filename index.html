Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment? ⌄

You can book online through this website by clicking "Book Appointment" or simply call us at 80589-93399. SMS confirmation is sent within 5 minutes.

Is the emergency service available 24×7? ⌄

Yes, our emergency and trauma care unit operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, all 365 days a year.

Do you provide ambulance service? ⌄

Yes, ambulance service is available 24×7. Call 82333-37879 for immediate dispatch.

What is the consultation fee? ⌄

Consultation fees range from ₹300 to ₹600 depending on the specialty. Emergency triage is free.

Do you accept insurance? ⌄

Yes, we accept various government health schemes and most major private insurance providers. Please call to verify your provider.

Is parking available? ⌄

Yes, free parking is available on hospital premises for patients and visitors.

Do you offer home visits? ⌄

Yes, home visits can be arranged for senior citizens and critically ill patients. Call our helpline to schedule.

How to check bed availability? ⌄

Real-time bed availability is shown on this website. For confirmed admission, call 80589-93399.
